\begin{enumerate}



\item $p$-regular graphs.

Let $p$ be a prime and $G=(V, E)$ a graph with average degree larger than $2p - 2$ and maximum degree at most $2p-1$.
Show that $G$ contains a $p$-regular subgraph.



\item Hopf-Stiefel condition

For integers $n, r, t$, we define $\beta = \beta_n(r, t)$ to be the smallest integer such that $\binom{\beta}{k}$ is a multiple of $n$ for all $k = \beta - r + 1, \ldots, s -1$.

Let $p$ be a prime, $t$ a positive integer and let $A, B \subseteq \F_p^t$ be non-empty subsets.
Then $|A + B| \geq \beta_p(|A|, |B|)$



\item List colouring

Show that for every $k$ there is an integer $n$ such that $\chi^{\ell}(K_{n, n}) \geq k$.

\end{enumerate}
